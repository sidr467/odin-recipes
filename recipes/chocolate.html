Chocolate Truffle

Description

These simple chocolate truffles have just three ingredients. It’s a must-try recipe that makes the easiest, most divine Christmas treat or gift. At first bite, they’re cool and firm. Then with the warmth of the tongue, they collapse into silky, melty bliss.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Cream
  • Buttter
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. All you have to do is bring some butter, chocolate and cream to a simmer
  2. Stop when the chocolate is 80% melted
  3. Simply stirring for a few minutes will melt everything together
  4. Let stand and after a couple hours, it will be perfectly scoopable
